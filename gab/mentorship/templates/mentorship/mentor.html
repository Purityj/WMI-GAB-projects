Welcome, Mentor!

Dear Mentor, thank you for taking the time to mentor another scholar. Your role in shaping the next generation of scholars is invaluable, and we deeply appreciate your dedication and expertise.

Explore below resources designed to assist you in your mentoring journey. These materials are here to guide you but not dictate your approach. Feel free to adapt them to suit your mentoring style and your mentee's goals. You can download the materials or view them directly in your browser.

If you have additional resources or materials that you believe would benefit your mentee, we encourage you to share them with your mentee.

Mentorship Schedule

Below is the schedule for the mentorship program, outlining key dates and the sessions to be covered on those dates.

The schedule is designed for biweekly meetings between mentors and mentees. However, in case of unforeseen circumstances, you may adjust the schedule to better suit your availability. Academic Track

Use these materials if your mentees is in Academic Track.

This track is for student scholars who are looking to excel in their academics and need guidance from mentors who have gone through the same academics and have excelled. and therefore are aware of dos and donts. Your main task as a mentor is to utilize these materials and any other you deem necessary to guide your mentees so that when they graduate they graduate with good grades, on time and with a clear view of what they would like to do or be.

Professional Track

Use these materials if your mentees is in Professional Track.

This track is designed for mentees looking for career guidance. You as the professional in the career the mentee is looking to join is expected to use these material and any other materials you deem necessary to provide guidance to your mentee(s) so that at the end of the mentorship your mentee should have skills in resume and cover letter writing, networking, interview, understanding workplace dynamics, and making informed decisions about their career progression.

By the end of this track, they should be better equipped to achieve their professional goals with confidence and clarity.
