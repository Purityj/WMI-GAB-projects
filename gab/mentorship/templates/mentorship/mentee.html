Welcome, Mentee!

Dear Mentors, thank you for taking the time out of your busy schedules to mentor another scholar. Your role in shaping the next generation of scholars is invaluable, and we deeply appreciate your dedication and expertise.

Explore resources designed to assist you in your mentoring journey. These materials are here to guide you but not dictate your approach. Feel free to adapt them to suit your mentoring style and your mentee's goals. You can download the materials or view them directly in your browser.

If you have additional resources or materials that you believe would benefit your mentee, we encourage you to share them with your mentee.
