About the Program

The Mentorship Program is a three months program during which student scholars receive personalized mentorship from graduate scholars who have successfully navigated their own academic and professional journeys. This program aims to foster a nurturing and supportive environment that promotes academic excellence, personal growth, and professional development.

How It Works

Matching Process

We meticulously match student scholars with graduate mentors based on mutual interests, academic majors, values, and hobbies. This personalized approach ensures a meaningful and productive mentorship experience.

Duration

The mentorship relationship lasts for three months, providing ample time for mentors and mentees to build a strong connection and achieve their goals.

Activities

  • Mentor and mentee to meet every 2 weeks
  • Career guidance
  • Academic support
  • Networking opportunities

Support

Both mentors and mentees receive continuous support from the Mentorship Program team to ensure a smooth and enriching experience.

Program Benefits

Guidance

Receive personalized advice and support tailored to your academic and career aspirations.

Networking

Build valuable connections with experienced professionals and fellow scholars.

Skill Development

Enhance your skills through targeted mentorship activities and resources.

Personal Growth

Gain confidence, improve your leadership abilities, and develop a clearer vision for your future.
